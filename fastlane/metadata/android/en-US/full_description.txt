FrameEcho - Freeze Every Frame You Love

FrameEcho is an Android app for video frame extraction and high-quality screenshot capture. Pick the exact frame you want, then export it as a still image or motion photo.

Best for
- Capturing perfect moments from travel videos, concerts, family clips, and short-form content.
- Saving clean, high-quality screenshots for sharing or archiving.
- Exporting motion photos to keep short moments alive.

Key features
- Precise frame picking with smooth timeline scrubbing.
- Still image and motion photo export.
- HDR-aware processing for HDR10, HDR10+, HLG, and Dolby Vision sources.
- EXIF metadata preservation where supported.
- Multiple output formats: JPEG, PNG, WebP, HEIF, AVIF.
- Adjustable quality and resolution limits.

Designed for speed and clarity
- On-demand thumbnail loading for responsive seeking.
- Streamlined export panel for format, quality, filename, and destination.
- Clean Material 3 interface with practical controls.

Privacy
- No account required.
- Files are processed when you explicitly choose and export media.
- Processing is designed to stay local on your device.

If you want a focused Android frame capture app with quality-first export options, FrameEcho is built for you.
